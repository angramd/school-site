About

Our Mission

Trezevant High School empowers our students with the knowledge, character, and experiences to achieve in college and beyond.

Vision

Trezevant High School will have moved from the bottom 5% to the top 25% of schools in Shelby County and Tennessee BY 2020.

Philosophy

Trezevant High School believes that with great teaching, high expectations and structured learning environment; all students will excel.
