Cordova Elementary

Our Mission

The mission of Cordova Elementary School is to provide a safe and challenging environment that is committed to helping all of our students successfully achieve their maximum potential. Students will communicate with excellent reading and writing skills, apply mathematics in realistic situations, and explore scientific concepts. Our parents, teachers, and community members work together to set demanding academic standards and encourage creative artistic expression in every student.
